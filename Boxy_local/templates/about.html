About Us

We are a team of passionate students from LJ University, dedicated to revolutionizing the parcel delivery industry through innovation and technology.

As part of our academic project, we've developed Boxy - a comprehensive parcel delivery platform that combines cutting-edge technology with exceptional service. Our mission is to make parcel delivery fast, reliable, and affordable for everyone.

Through this project, we aim to demonstrate how technology can solve real-world problems and create value for both customers and delivery partners. We believe that every package matters, and we treat each delivery with the care and attention it deserves.
